CORPORATE BUDGET POLICY 2024
============================

1. INTRODUCTION
This document establishes the budget policies and procedures for fiscal year 2024.

2. BUDGET APPROVAL PROCESS
- All department budgets must be submitted by November 15th
- CFO review and consolidation by December 1st
- Board approval required by December 15th
- Final budgets distributed by January 1st

3. EXPENSE CATEGORIES

3.1 Personnel Costs
- Salary increases capped at 5% for existing roles
- New positions require ROI justification
- Bonus pool allocation: 10% of operating profit

3.2 Capital Expenditures
- Projects over $50,000 require board approval
- IT infrastructure budget: $500,000
- Equipment replacement: $200,000

3.3 Operating Expenses
- Travel: Maximum $5,000 per employee annually
- Training: $2,000 per employee annually
- Marketing: 8% of projected revenue

4. VARIANCE REPORTING
- Monthly variance reports due by 5th business day
- Variances over 10% require written explanation
- Quarterly reviews with executive team

5. BUDGET AMENDMENTS
- Minor adjustments (under 5%) approved by department head
- Major adjustments require CFO approval
- Emergency spending requires CEO approval

6. KEY FINANCIAL TARGETS
- Revenue Growth: 15%
- Operating Margin: 25%
- EBITDA Margin: 30%
- Cash Conversion Cycle: 45 days
